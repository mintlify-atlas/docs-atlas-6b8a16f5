Carnero.Dev is a student-driven, community-supported event. The prize structure is intentionally designed to motivate skill development, portfolio building, and engineering growth — not just winning. Every team that ships a complete project and delivers a live demo walks away with something tangible, whether that is cash, hardware, a certificate, or a special recognition that carries real weight in a professional portfolio.

## Prize Details

<Note>
  All monetary prizes are a single lump-sum payment per team — not distributed per individual member. Prizes are delivered in person immediately after the judge panel completes its deliberation at the closing ceremony on Sunday Oct 18.
</Note>

## Special Recognition Awards

Beyond the top-three podium, Carnero.Dev presents two special recognition awards that honor outstanding technical creativity and design quality independently of overall ranking.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Mención de Innovación Disruptiva" icon="lightbulb" href="/prizes-and-recognition">
    Physical medal and digital badge for the team presenting the most audacious software architecture or technical integration of the event. Judged on boldness of approach, technical depth, and real-world applicability.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Mención de Diseño & UX" icon="paintbrush" href="/prizes-and-recognition">
    Special recognition for the team that delivers the most aesthetic, usable, and user-centered digital product. Evaluated on visual design quality, interaction flow, accessibility, and overall user experience.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Additional Sponsor Prizes

<Info>
  Negotiations are actively ongoing with local and regional sponsors to add software licenses, hardware kits, and in-kind prizes to the award pool. All additional prizes will be announced publicly alongside full sponsor reveal announcements on the official Carnero.Dev site and Discord server.
</Info>

<Warning>
  Prizes — including cash awards, certificates, special mentions, and sponsor gifts — are only awarded to teams that complete a live demo and deliver their final pitch at the closing ceremony on Sunday Oct 18. Teams that do not present will be excluded from all prize categories regardless of code quality.
</Warning>

Carnero.Dev is a student-driven, community-supported event. The prize structure is intentionally designed to motivate skill development, portfolio building, and engineering growth — not just winning. Every team that ships a complete project and delivers a live demo walks away with something tangible, whether that is cash, a certificate, or a special recognition that carries real weight in a professional portfolio.

## Special Recognition Awards

Beyond the top-three podium, Carnero.Dev presents special recognition awards that honor outstanding technical creativity and design quality independently of overall ranking.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Mención de Innovación Disruptiva" icon="lightbulb" href="/prizes-and-recognition">
    Physical medal and digital badge for the team presenting the most audacious software architecture or technical integration of the event. Judged on boldness of approach, technical depth, and real-world applicability.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Mención de Diseño & UX" icon="paintbrush" href="/prizes-and-recognition">
    Special recognition for the team that delivers the most aesthetic, usable, and user-centered digital product. Evaluated on visual design quality, interaction flow, and overall user experience.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

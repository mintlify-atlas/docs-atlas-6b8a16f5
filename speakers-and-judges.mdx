Carnero.Dev brings together experienced industry professionals in three distinct roles: guest speakers who lead the Friday workshops, technical mentors who guide teams through the 36-hour hacking sprint, and independent judges who score final presentations on Demo Day. Announcements will be made progressively as the event approaches and confirmations are finalized.

## Roles at the Event

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Guest Speakers" icon="microphone" href="/speakers-and-judges">
    Industry professionals delivering express technical sessions on AI, cloud, DevOps, and security during the Friday morning workshop block. Sessions are short, dense, and immediately actionable.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Technical Mentors" icon="chalkboard-teacher" href="/speakers-and-judges">
    Experts available throughout the full 36-hour hacking period (Fri 12 PM – Sat 11:59 PM). Scheduled mentoring rounds run every 6 hours, covering both technical direction and business strategy.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Competition Judges" icon="gavel" href="/speakers-and-judges">
    An independent panel that scores all final project presentations on Demo Day. Judges evaluate submissions across the event's defined judging criteria. See the [evaluation criteria](/evaluation-criteria) page for the full scoring rubric.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Confirmed Specialties

The event is recruiting professionals from the following four specialty areas, ensuring judges and mentors cover the full technical breadth of the competition tracks:

- **Software Development & Architecture** — system design, scalable backends, API-first development, and engineering best practices
- **Artificial Intelligence & Data** — applied ML, LLM integrations, data pipelines, and responsible AI deployment
- **Cloud, DevOps & Infrastructure** — rapid cloud deployments, CI/CD pipelines, containerization, and infrastructure as code
- **Cybersecurity & Robust Systems** — secure software design, threat modeling, authentication patterns, and resilient architectures
